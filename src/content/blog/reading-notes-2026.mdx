Some quick notes from recent reading, mostly on systems thinking and habits.

## Systems over goals

Goals set a direction; systems are what you actually repeat. The book that
hammered this home for me was *Atomic Habits* — the idea that identity change
is upstream of behavior change stuck with me.

## Compounding is boring in the middle

Every book on long-term thinking says the same thing in different words: the
payoff is invisible for a long time before it isn't. Patience is the actual
skill, not the strategy.
